This anniversary is about you

It all started with a tiny satellite.

Twenty years ago, a close cooperation between EU countries and the European Commission culminated into the launch of Spot-4 VEGETATION. A spacecraft that would go on to improve our understanding of the world and its ecosystems.

VITO Remote Sensing took off as the image processing centre for the mission, translating earth observation images to new data and insights.

But much more than the start of our remote sensing activities, March 24 1998 was a celebration of cooperation and knowledge sharing in general. A day that was much less about tech, than about the people behind it. This anniversary really is about you.

Join us to celebrate this occasion together with your peers.
